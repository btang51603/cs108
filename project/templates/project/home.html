About Cards and Rentals

    Cards and Rentals is dedicated towards Magic The Gathering players who play Modern but don't have the wallet to buy a deck or aren't sure if they want to commit to buying a deck. Cards and Rentals helps send decks to players for players to use for a limited time a much cheaper price using our card inventory or to help players new to the Modern format to get a taste of it.
